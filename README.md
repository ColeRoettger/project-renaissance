# project-renaissance
Project Renaissance is the public record of becoming the engineer I want to be—not through talent or shortcuts, but through disciplined, consistent execution. Every commit represents another step away from who I was yesterday and toward the future I'm intentionally building.


For years I built my career in the electrical power industry as a NETA field technician. While that experience taught me discipline, problem solving, and technical troubleshooting, I realized I wanted to build a future around technology, engineering, and creating value from a computer rather than in the field.

This repository documents that transformation.

The goal of Project Renaissance is to become a world-class technology professional by mastering the foundations of computer science, cybersecurity, cloud computing, software development, artificial intelligence, and automation.

Rather than chasing shortcuts or collecting certificates without understanding, this project focuses on building real technical competence through consistent daily practice, hands-on projects, public documentation, and continuous learning.

Every project, note, script, lab, certification, and lesson learned will be documented here.

This repository serves as both my learning journal and proof of work.

The long-term vision extends far beyond earning my first role in tech.

My goals are to:

Transition from NETA field work into a full-time technology career.
Build expertise in cybersecurity, cloud engineering, Python, Linux, networking, and automation.
Develop an elite portfolio of real-world projects that demonstrates practical engineering ability.
Earn respected certifications while developing skills that go beyond certifications alone.
Learn software development fundamentals to become capable of building production-quality applications.
Use artificial intelligence responsibly to automate workflows, solve business problems, and create products.
Build a personal brand by documenting the journey publicly.
Create multiple streams of income through technical expertise, software, AI, education, and entrepreneurship.
Continue learning throughout my career with the mindset that mastery is never finished.

Project Renaissance is not simply about changing careers.

It is about building a completely new identity centered around curiosity, discipline, continuous improvement, and creating technology that makes an impact.
